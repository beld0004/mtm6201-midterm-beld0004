Who is John Ojerio?

John Ojerio is the director, videographer and cinematographer of Black Toro Productions

He started his love of films from a young age of 6.

He decided to take film more serious when he decided to enroll for college. Thus, he has already directed 1 commercial, 2 short films along with a documentary he produced and directed.
